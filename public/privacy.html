Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2025

Berb is a peer-to-peer file transfer platform that enables users to send files securely and directly from one device to another using WebRTC technology. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Berb.

1. Overview

Berb is designed to maximize user privacy. All file transfers are conducted directly between users using end-to-end encrypted WebRTC connections. Files are never stored on our servers, and Berb does not require an account to use the core functionality of the app.

2. Information We Collect

While Berb does not store files or collect personal information for transfers, we do collect certain types of data to maintain the service and improve user experience.

2.1 Signaling Data

To initiate a peer-to-peer connection, Berb uses a temporary signaling server to exchange network metadata (such as IP addresses, session descriptions, and ICE candidates). This information is:

  • Ephemeral (not stored or logged)
  • Used solely to establish the connection between peers
  • Deleted immediately after the connection is established

2.2 Analytics Data

We use analytics tools such as Google Analytics, Vercel Analytics, or similar services to gather anonymous usage data, including:

  • Page views and session duration
  • Browser and device types
  • Interaction events (e.g., file sent, transfer completed)
  • Geographical location (country-level)

This data helps us improve the app, fix bugs, and understand how users interact with Berb. Analytics data is collected in aggregate and does not include file contents or identifying personal information.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Berb may use cookies or localStorage for essential site functionality, such as remembering user settings or session state. We do not use cookies to track users across other websites.

4. File Transfers

All file transfers through Berb use WebRTC technology, which includes built-in end-to-end encryption (DTLS/SRTP). Files are streamed directly from the sender to the receiver and are never uploaded, cached, or stored on any intermediary server operated by Berb.

5. Data Retention

Since Berb does not store user files or account data, there is no persistent personal data to retain. Analytics data may be retained according to the policies of the third-party providers used (e.g., Google Analytics). We do not retain signaling metadata after the connection is established.

6. Third-Party Services

Berb may rely on third-party services for analytics, infrastructure, or error reporting. These may include:

  • Google Analytics
  • Vercel (hosting and serverless functions)
  • Cloudflare (for content delivery and DDoS protection)

These providers may collect anonymized data as part of their service delivery. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the integrity and confidentiality of your data. WebRTC connections are encrypted, and we do not store transmitted files. However, as with any internet-based service, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Berb is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Your continued use of Berb constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
